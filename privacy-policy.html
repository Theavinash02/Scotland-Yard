Playing without an account

You can play fully without signing in. Your settings, game history, and progress are then stored only on your device (browser local storage) and are never sent anywhere.

Google sign-in (optional)

If you choose "Sign in with Google," we receive your Google account identifier, display name, and profile photo via Firebase Authentication. We use this to sync your display name, game history, achievements, and your "remove ads" purchase status across your devices. We do not sell this data or use it for advertising targeting.

Data we store when signed in

  • Display name, game history (win/loss records), and achievements.
  • Your "no ads" entitlement flag (whether you purchased ad removal).

This is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) under your account and is readable/writable only by you. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us.

Advertising

Free users may see ads at natural breaks between games. Ads are served by Google AdMob (mobile apps) and Google AdSense (web), which may use advertising identifiers. See Google's advertising privacy information at policies.google.com/technologies/ads. Purchasing "Remove ads" disables all advertising.

Purchases

The "Remove ads" purchase is processed by Google Play Billing or the Apple App Store; we never receive your payment details. We store only whether the entitlement is active.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Your choices

You may play signed-out, sign out at any time, request data deletion, and remove ads via purchase. Clearing site data / uninstalling removes all local data.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be noted here with a new date.
